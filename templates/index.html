Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AutoClip Flow

How does the AI know which video segments to extract?

Our AI analyzes video content using advanced computer vision and audio processing algorithms. It identifies engaging moments based on visual interest, audio peaks, and content relevance patterns learned from millions of videos.

Is my data and content secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption for all data transmission and storage. Your social media credentials are encrypted and never stored in plain text. We comply with GDPR and SOC 2 security standards.

Which platforms do you support?

We currently support YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Vimeo, and Facebook. We're constantly adding support for new platforms based on user demand. Contact us if you need support for a specific platform.

Can I customize the automation workflows?

Yes! Our platform offers both pre-built templates and a visual workflow builder. You can customize extraction rules, output formats, storage destinations, and create complex multi-step automation pipelines.

What's included in the free trial?

The 14-day free trial includes full access to all features, unlimited video processing, and priority support. No credit card required. You can process up to 100 videos during the trial period.
